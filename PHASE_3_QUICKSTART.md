# ⚡ Phase 3 Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 What's New in Phase 3

Your ReadFlow app now has **Intelligent Analysis + Personalization** system complete with:
- 📊 Diagnostic test to assess your reading profile
- 📋 Auto-generated personalized training plans
- 📈 Progress tracking with charts

---

## 🚀 How to Access Phase 3 Features

### 1. **Diagnostic Test** (Start Here!)
```
Navigate to: Centro de Entrenamiento → Diagnóstico
```

**What it does:**
- Asks 4 quick questions about your reading habits
- Identifies your learning style (visual/auditory/kinesthetic/reading-writing)
- Analyzes your strengths and weaknesses
- Generates personalized recommendations

**Time**: ~5 minutes

**Output**: Complete profile with:
- Reading Speed (WPM)
- Comprehension level
- Focus level
- Peripheral Vision potential
- Working Memory capacity

---

### 2. **Personalized Plan** (Auto-Generated!)
```
Navigate to: Centro de Entrenamiento → Plan Personalizado
```

**What you get:**
- 7-day weekly schedule tailored to your profile
- Daily routine recommendations (60-75 min)
- Monthly goals with progress tracking
- Automatic plan adaptation rules

**Example Weekly Schedule:**
- **Monday**: Lectura + Comprensión (65 min)
- **Tuesday**: Memoria + Velocidad (60 min)
- **Wednesday**: Active Rest (45 min)
- **Thursday**: Balanced Focus (65 min)
- **Friday**: Progress Test (65 min)
- **Saturday**: Intense Training (80 min)
- **Sunday**: Complete Rest (30 min)

---

### 3. **Progress Tracking**
```
Navigate to: Centro de Entrenamiento → Progreso
```

**Visualize:**
- % improvement in speed, comprehension, focus, vision, memory
- Bar charts comparing current vs. goal metrics
- Detailed metric breakdown

---

### 4. **Download Certificates**
```
Navigate to: Centro de Entrenamiento → Certificados
```

**Certificate Types:**
- 🏆 **Level Certificate**: When you advance levels
- 📈 **Progress Certificate**: When you improve significantly
- ⭐ **Achievement Certificate**: For completing badges
- 🎉 **Annual Summary**: Yearly statistics

**Download as**: HTML (ready to print or share)

## 💡 How Personalization Works

### Adaptive Plan
- **If you score >80% accuracy**: Plan increases intensity by 10%
- **If you score <50% accuracy**: Plan reduces intensity by 10%
- **If you advance a level**: Plan increases complexity

### Automatic Recommendations
Based on your diagnostic results, you get specific recommendations:
- Visual learners → Visual guides and charts
- Auditory learners → Use text-to-speech
- Low comprehension → Focus on comprehension tests
- Weak memory → Extra N-Back training

## 📱 Tips for Best Results

### Follow the Plan
- Don't skip the "Descanso" (rest) periods - your brain needs them
- Consistency matters more than intensity
- 5-6 days per week is ideal, 3+ minimum

### Use Your Learning Style
- Visual: Focus on Schulte table exercises
- Auditory: Try text-to-speech or read aloud
- Kinesthetic: Take notes while reading
- Reading/Writing: Do comprehension tests after each session

### Track Your Progress
- Check "Progreso" tab weekly
- Notice improvements in speed AND comprehension
- Download certificates as motivation

### Adapt Intelligently
- If accuracy drops below 50%, slow down voluntarily
- If you breeze through, the plan auto-increases difficulty
- Monthly goals are guidelines, not rigid targets

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I change my learning style?**
A: Yes, restart the diagnostic test anytime to update your profile.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: No problem! The plan adapts. Just continue from where you left off.

**Q: How do I download certificates?**
A: Certificates auto-generate when you achieve milestones. Go to "Certificados" tab and click "Descargar".

**Q: Can I print certificates?**
A: Yes! Download as HTML, open in browser, and use browser's print function.

**Q: How often should I retake the diagnostic?**
A: Every 30 days to measure progress and adapt the plan.

**Q: What if my comprehension is low?**
A: The plan automatically adds more comprehension tests and slows your speed target.
